Privacy Policy — Data Collection and Retention

Konjo AI collects only the data required to deliver the Service: account email,
billing address, and the content of API requests you submit. We retain request
payloads for thirty (30) days for debugging and abuse detection, after which
they are deleted from primary storage and rotated out of backups within an
additional sixty (60) days.

Under the GDPR (EU 2016/679) and the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have
the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise
these rights, email privacy@konjo.ai with a verifiable request. We respond
within thirty (30) calendar days.

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with sub-processors listed in
the Trust Center: Stripe (billing), Cloudflare (DDoS), and AWS (hosting).

For questions, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@konjo.ai.
