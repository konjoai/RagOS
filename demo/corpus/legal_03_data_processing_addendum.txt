Data Processing Addendum (DPA) — Controller and Processor Obligations

This Addendum supplements the Master Services Agreement and governs the
processing of Personal Data by Konjo AI ("Processor") on behalf of the
Customer ("Controller") in compliance with Article 28 of the GDPR.

Scope: Processor will process Personal Data only for the documented purpose
of providing the Service, and only in accordance with Controller's lawful
written instructions. Categories of data processed: identifiers, technical
metadata, and the content of API requests submitted by Controller.

Security: Processor implements appropriate technical and organizational
measures including encryption at rest (AES-256), encryption in transit
(TLS 1.3+), role-based access controls, immutable audit logs, and SOC 2
Type II controls. Processor will notify Controller of any Personal Data
breach without undue delay, and in any event within seventy-two (72) hours
of becoming aware of the breach.

Sub-processors: Processor may engage sub-processors only with Controller's
prior general written authorization. The current list is published in the
Trust Center; Controller will be notified of additions at least 30 days
in advance.
